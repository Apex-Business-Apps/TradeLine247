This Action is Permanent

Once you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account credentials and login information
  • All personal details (name, email, phone, company)
  • Call history and conversation transcripts
  • AI training data derived from your interactions
  • Account preferences and settings

This cannot be undone. However, you have a 30-day grace period to reactivate your account.
