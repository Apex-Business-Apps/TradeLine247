1. Information Collection

What personal information we collect and why

We collect information to provide our AI receptionist services and improve your experience. We only collect what's necessary for our core functionality.

When you create an account or use our services, we collect:

  • Name and contact details: To personalize your experience and route calls correctly
  • Email address: For account management, notifications, and password recovery
  • Phone number: Essential for our AI receptionist to handle incoming calls
  • Company information: To provide industry-specific service improvements

To improve our AI and service quality, we collect:

  • Call logs and transcripts: Content of conversations with our AI receptionist
  • Interaction timestamps: When calls occur and how long they last
  • Service usage patterns: Which features you use most frequently
  • Performance metrics: Call success rates and AI response quality

We automatically collect technical information to ensure service reliability:

  • Device information: Operating system, app version, device type
  • IP address: For security and fraud prevention (not stored long-term)
  • App usage statistics: Crash reports and performance data
  • Location data: Only business address you provide (never real-time tracking)

2. How We Use Your Information

Purposeful use of your data to provide excellent service

Your information helps us provide, maintain, and improve our AI receptionist services. We use data responsibly and only for these specific purposes:

  • AI Receptionist Services: Process and route customer calls, generate responses, and provide 24/7 availability
  • Call Processing: Analyze conversation content to provide accurate, helpful responses
  • Service Improvement: Use anonymized patterns to enhance AI accuracy and response quality
  • Communication: Send call summaries, notifications, and important service updates
  • Account Management: Process registrations, handle support requests, and maintain security
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and protect against fraud

Important: We never sell your personal data to third parties or use it for marketing purposes without explicit consent.

7. Children's Privacy

Our commitment to protecting young users

TradeLine 24/7 is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 18 years of age.

  • No collection: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18
  • Parental rights: Parents or guardians may request deletion of any discovered child data
  • Verification: Age verification may be required for certain features
  • COPPA compliance: We follow Children's Online Privacy Protection Act requirements

If we discover we've collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately and notify the parent or guardian.
